
# Chapter 1: The Sovereign's Desolation

The Page was neither paper nor digital canvas. It was The White Before Light—The Absolute ∅. It existed as the primal, unwritten source: the Creator of Supreme Creators. This was a realm completely beyond the tiering system, entirely detached from any concept of levels, dimensions, scaling, or real-world limitations. Before human imagination could ever box reality into fiction, this formless, absolute purity was all that existed.

In the dead center of this boundless canvas stood Divine Goddess Arora, the Supreme Creator and the almighty manifestation of the Origin itself. Directly beside her stood Wang Xuan, the Pillar of Immutable Law. His power level scaled to a terrifying, absolute True Boundless Tier 0, placing him almost entirely near to the ultimate level of Divine Goddess Arora herself.

Surrounding them were five other entities arrayed in a perfect geometric circle. Together, they were the 7 Pillars of Infinite Eternity—an assembly of apex authorities existing completely beyond the concept of limits. To these entities, reality was a plaything. Each of the True Boundless Pillars possessed the absolute authority to create infinite eternities within 0.0 seconds, and destroy those same infinite eternities just as instantly with a single thought.

Eons passed in the automated order of the cosmic timelines, but order is a fragile illusion to a True Boundless entity. One day, without warning, Wang Xuan turned against the hierarchy. He breached the highest sanctuaries of The White Before Light and ascended into the ultimate heaven of the gods.

He did not return to maintain order. He returned for blood.

Standing at the epicenter of the high heavens, Wang Xuan unleashed his full Tier 0 True Boundless wrath. Before him materialized an army of unimaginable proportions—1000 crore septillion heavenly gods, an endless ocean of True Boundless, True Immortal deities whose collective presence should have crushed reality itself.

Wang Xuan did not flinch. A cold, mocking smile touched his lips.

"Come at me, all of you," Wang Xuan whispered.

Within a fraction of 0.0 seconds, he moved. He tore through the immortal army brutally and ruthlessly. True Immortal gods were ripped apart like paper. He shattered their domains with simple backhands, crushing their eternal souls into non-existence. Trillions of deities disintegrated per millisecond. When the dust settled, the remaining six Pillars of Infinite Eternity watched from the shadows. Wang Xuan turned his blood-stained gaze toward them. But his absolute law was not meant for them. He had no intention of killing the six Pillars. They stood untouched.

Instead, Wang Xuan looked straight up into the formless, blinding purity of the supreme origin itself. He threw his arms out, his voice roaring like a conceptual cataclysm:

"Come! Where are you?!"

The blinding purity of the unwritten void rippled violently.

"Why do you want to fight with me?!" The White Before Light questioned, its tone vibrating with the confusion of a primal source witnessing its own creation rebel.

Wang Xuan smirked. "Just come, you false god! And I'll become the Absolute Creator!"

The voice of the primal source returned, dropping to a cold, transactional tone. "In one condition... I'll go against you... if you win against my Created Army."

Instantly, the unwritten void split wide open. The White Before Light created 80 trillion septillion Gods of Death near to Wang Xuan's level. Each one possessed a power level scaling almost exactly near to his True Boundless Tier 0 level.

The White Before Light commanded: "Now, Wang Xuan, they will give you the absolute death."

But Wang Xuan only smiled. And Round 1 started.

Within a fraction of 0.0 seconds, he unleashed his absolute wrath. He ruthlessly and brutally killed every single God of Death. He caught their scythes with his bare hands, shattering them into dust. He systematically dismantled their infinite formations, erasing their very names until the entire 80 trillion septillion army was wiped out.

Wang Xuan looked down at the drifting ashes. "Stand proud," he said colds. "You all make me feel better." He had won round 1.

Now it's time for Round 2.

The White Before Light did not respond with anger. Instead, the white light created the absolute power: the God of Illusions, truly equal to Wang Xuan.

The God of Illusions used its absolute illusion on him, trapping his mind in a perfect, unbreakable reality distortion. But Wang Xuan was not bound by the logic of the canvas. With a silent roar, Wang Xuan broke the rules of all Creations and gods—he crossed his own infinite powers. He forced his Tier 0 nature to multiply by infinity. The illusion cracked like cheap glass. Wang Xuan lunged forward, grabbed the God of Illusions by its conceptual face, and killed it ruthlessly and brutally, tearing its essence into permanently erased nothingness.

From the safe perimeter, Goddess Arora was feeling bad about Wang Xuan because she's in love with him. Her golden eyes were heavy with pain. The other five Pillars stood in absolute shock, watching the fight. One of them muttered in terror, "He's the father of all badass, the absolute evil."

Wang Xuan heard them. He turned his head, his pitch-black grin spreading. "If you think I'm absolute evil, then I'll become more."

He looked into the middle of the battle straight into Arora's golden eyes. His blazing blue eyes could see her pain. He saw the deep, shattering ache of the woman who loved him. Yet, he did not stop.

Wang Xuan raised his hand and spoke his ultimate execution method: "1st step: Almighty Divine Flame... 2nd step: The Absolute Death." A conceptual fire erupted, followed by a pitch-black shockwave of definitive erasure. And they all dead. Every remaining guardian and barrier vanished.

And Round 3 starts. He's laughing.

The White Before Light pulled from the absolute zenith of its unwritten power. In Round 3, the White Before Light created 100 septillion gods and evil gods, True Tier 0 and more powerful than Wang Xuan.

The collision was apocalyptic. The whole nothingness, timelines, universe, meta-fiction, or non-fiction were completely destroyed because of Wang Xuan. Even the true narrative level was destroyed, alongside the whole entire thing or non-things.

But the tragedy struck. Wang Xuan couldn't even kill or defeat anyone because they were very powerful. All the gods and evil gods were dominating Wang Xuan in the fight. They broke his body, shattering his conceptual armor.

Seeing the man she loved overpowered, Goddess Arora tried to fight with Wang Xuan. She stepped forward to unleash her supreme creator essence. But Wang Xuan stopped her. He had already chosen his death, but he rejected defeat. He spat out golden blood and roared, "Don't interfere in my fight!" because he knew how powerful all the gods were. He refused to beg for help.

Wang Xuan was almost near to death. All the evil gods and gods surrounded him, demanding: "Accept your loss and come under the White Before Light!"

Wang Xuan looked straight into the blinding source. "I reject them both..."

With his final embers of life, Wang Xuan yelled, "I summon the Sword of Absolute End!" A weapon of definitive termination manifested in his hand. Simultaneously, he opened his six wings of blazing blue and pitch-black energy.

"4th step: The Infinite Domain of Sword," he declared. An inescapable space of absolute cutting edges locked the 100 septillion deities inside.

Wang Xuan became a killing machine of gods, the true nightmare of gods. He abandoned his mind, moving with mechanical precision. He killed them all ruthlessly and brutally, and he destroyed the absolute ∅. He slammed his ultimate blade down, shattering the primal zero-point. He even destroyed all the absolute mathematical real-world logics, wiping away numbers, geometry, and equations entirely.

Standing in the logicless blankness, he said, "I won all 3 rounds."

Suddenly, the voice of the primal source manifested from the ruined void. "You broke all limitations and the absolute mathematical logics. You destroy ∅... but you are just a dying ant against me."

Hearing the source dismiss his ultimate victory so effortlessly, all the original Pillars were completely shocked.

Arora looked at his eyes. In his eyes, there was no fear. Wang Xuan looked into the void and said to himself, "I'm the absolute ruler."

The White Before Light delivered its poetic execution sentence: "You gonna masterpiece to rest in peace!"

At those words, Arora was broken... her heart... dying. Her divine composure shattered completely.

Wang Xuan looked up at the closing white walls and threw his final insult: "The White Before Light, come, shit."

The primal source responded with an absolute compression. "I'm sealing your all memories, and I'm sealing your powers inner side in you." And in the end, without a fight, the White Before Light sealed his powers and memories.

Yet, the source spoke one last decree: "I'll give you titles: The Absolute Ruler. And you and Arora are the one more beautiful than anything, or everything, or nothing, or nothingness."

Now, Arora came and hugged Wang Xuan as he was losing his powers and memories. She cradled his numbing body. She looked up at the primal source with the raw authority of her golden eyes. Under her final, piercing glare, the White Before Light instantly gone.

But before the White Before Light gone from the battle, he sealed the 6 Pillars' power and memories, and he instantly created and destroyed the whole heaven, infinite eternity, nothing, nothingness, timelines, and everything within 0.0 sec.

And his final command echoed to the sealed, memory-wiped entities: "U all go in Earth, start life."

He cast the six Pillars down to the dirt of a newborn planet, forcing them into mortal shells. Arora, however, did not fall. Arora is part of the White Before Light. She remained suspended in the quiet cosmic origin, watching the man she loved fall to Earth, her golden eyes carrying the eternal secrets of the cosmos.
